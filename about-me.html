Hello, I am Mustafa Isikli !

I am the creator of Project Clash Royale - a hobby website. This project serves as a training area for me to test my self-taught web development skills and to improve them at the same time.

I have an affinity for sorting out data and putting those pieces of data into categories. Typing on a keyboard and seeing those visual changes on the screen brings joy into my eyes and it feels like playing a game for me.

Currently I am attending Dr. Angela Yu's online course in which I learned HTML5 and CSS3, furthermore I keep grinding the course and right now I am in the beginning of learning JavaScript.
